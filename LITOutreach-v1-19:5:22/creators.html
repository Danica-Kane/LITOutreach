Creators

Meet the independent creators promoting their work on LITOutreach. Creators include artists/illustrators and authors. Check out the gallery to see some of there work. Click on the creators profile picture to veiw their profile.

Danica Kane

Founder of LITOutreach, Author

Danica Kane is the founder and programer of LITOutreach. Additionally she is the author of LITOutreach's two featured novels - Reflection and Flares - as well as co-author of The Timeless Series with Ettiene Shaw, which is currently published under their joint LITOutreach account bearing the same name as the series.

Bentley

Illustrator

Bentley is an illustrator and creator of a series of original characters, including a cartoon version of himself. LITOutreach features two of his works, one electronic and one physical, both of which incorporate just one of his many art styles.

Ettiene

Author

Ettiene Shaw is an independent author. His works to date include several novels currently in production while in the public eye. Besides this he is also the co-author of The Timeless Series with Danica Kane and shares with her the LITOutreach creator account under the same name.

Timeless Series

Authors

The joint account of Danica Kane and Ettiene Shaw, dedicated specifically to their novel series currently still in production. The Timeless Series. The adventure/fantasy/thriller series follows the escapades of two alien children situated on Earth as they discover their heritage and strive to save it from the threat of humanity. Their life on the lam leads them to form friendships and make enemies, creating a series that is enthralling in how the reader finds themselves easily sucked into this new world.
