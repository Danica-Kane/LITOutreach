Publishing?

As an independent creator striving to either professionally publish or advertise your book/art to any degree of semi professionalism you'll notice it can be difficult to find resources to aid you in typesetting and printing your books or promoting your art on your own. Not to mention uploading you work as electronic products you can profit from. At LITOutreach we have compiled our favourite book formatters, literary networks for writing/editing/illustrating, printers, and guides to aid you in taking your work from publicly available art and ruff manuscripts to full products.
