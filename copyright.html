Copyrighting?

As an independent creator you alone are responsible for ensuring the safety and security of your work on this site. Whether that be through outside sources or done yourself. Copyrighting your work or applying Creative Commons is one way of ensuring others can't take your work and post it anywhere else or profit from it without your permission. Here at LIT Outreach we are not liable for ensuring you apply such things. We recommend you get acquainted with the following resources to begin learning about Copyright and Creative Commons. Choose what works best for you and think carefully before putting out your work without such protective measures in place.



https://creativecommons.org

to learn about creative commons

to learn about how to apply creative commons
