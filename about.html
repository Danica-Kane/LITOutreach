About






LITOutreach is the product of a semester long high school computer science project that incorporated a will to design a something unique and profession and the needs of young independent creators both within and outside the school. Creators simply submit content, and LITOutreach takes care of the rest. Approving works and uploading them to our promotional gallery with links to the content on its host platform itself. Profiles (located on a separate page to the gallery) mean creators get the full credit they deserve, and resources (conveniently located under the section labeled as such) enable them with the tools to protect their work. Individuals can connect and collaborate, reaching out to other users of the site, and get their work out to readers in a professional manner. Here we aim to nurture creativity, and inspire independent creators to pursue their passions in a supportive environment.

If your interested in submitting your work to LITOutreach head to our “Contact” page and get in touch.

- Danica Kane (Creator of LITOutreach)
